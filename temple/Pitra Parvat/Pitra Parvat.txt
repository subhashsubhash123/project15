Pitra Parvat

In the year 2002, The mayor of Indore Kailash Vijayvargiya got the idea of installing the biggest statue of Hanumanji at Devdharam Tekri.itra Parvat is not just an important religious spot; it has breathtaking panoramas of Indore. The mountain is located on the top, it is a peaceful retreat with lush vegetation. People do not just seek spiritual peace but also appreciate the natural beauty.
