Digambar jain Kanch Mandir 

Jain temple lavishly decorated with glass mosaics & chandeliers, plus an onyx Mahavira statue.A Jain temple adorning Indore's Itwaria Bazaar, it was built by Sir Seth Hukumchand Jain, a preeminent Indian industrialist and trader. Glistening in glass, the foundation stone of the temple was said to be laid in 1903 and completed in July 1921.


Kaanch Mandir, Pasari Bhavan, Opposite Kaanch Mandir, Itwaria Bazaar, Indore, Madhya Pradesh 452002
