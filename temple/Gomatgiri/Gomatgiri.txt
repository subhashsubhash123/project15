Gomatgiri

Gomatgiri is a sacred place for the devotees of the Jain religion (Digambar sect) ! Located at just a 5-minute drive away from the Indore airport, this small hillock is home to a 21 feet statue of Gomateshwar ! This statue is a replica of the Bahubali statue, which stands tall at Shravanabelagola !Hindu temple with statues on a serene hilltop offering scenic views of the city.
